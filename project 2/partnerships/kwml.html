KWML

  • General Info about KWML

    Kochi Water Metro Limited (KWML) is a government-owned company responsible for developing and managing the Kochi Water Metro project, which aims to provide an integrated water-based public transportation system in Kochi, Kerala. This innovative project complements the existing Kochi Metro rail system, offering a seamless and efficient transit solution that leverages the city's extensive network of waterways. KWML has partnered with Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology (RSET) to enhance the development and implementation of the Water Metro project. This collaboration focuses on research and development, technological innovation, and the practical application of sustainable transit solutions.
