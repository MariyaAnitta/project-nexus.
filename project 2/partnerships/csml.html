CSML

  • General Info about CSML

    Cochin Smart Mission Limited (CSML) is a special purpose vehicle established to implement the Smart City Mission in Kochi, Kerala. As part of the Indian government's broader Smart Cities Mission, CSML aims to drive economic growth and enhance the quality of life in Kochi by leveraging technology and local development. The primary objective of CSML is to transform Kochi into a sustainable and livable city through various smart initiatives that focus on enhancing infrastructure, improving public services, and promoting sustainable urban development.CSML's vision includes creating a city that uses technology to provide better governance, efficient public services, and a higher quality of life for its residents. Key projects and initiatives under CSML include the improvement of Kochi's transportation network, integrating various modes of transport, and promoting non-motorized transport options. The development of smart infrastructure is also a major focus, encompassing water supply systems, waste management, and energy-efficient public lighting. Additionally, CSML is implementing e-governance solutions to make public services more accessible and efficient for citizens, alongside urban planning projects aimed at improving the overall livability and sustainability of the city.
